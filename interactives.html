Arizona Legislative Compass

Explore every vote cast by Arizona lawmakers: The Legislative Compass is an interactive dashboard that helps you spot patterns in partisan loyalty, crossover votes, and how legislators compare to the politics and demographics of their districts. Filter by year, chamber, party, or district. The tool is designed for journalists and anyone curious about which lawmakers break ranks, who represents swing districts, and how lawmakers' voting records align with their electorate. Split Communities: Jacksonville Neighborhoods Map

Explore how Jacksonville City Council’s redistricting plan splits 47 neighborhoods across multiple districts—often dividing communities and weakening their political power. This interactive map lets you see which neighborhoods are split and how district lines cross community boundaries, using the city’s official neighborhood definitions. Based on reporting by The Tributary, the tool visualizes concerns about racial gerrymandering, diluted representation, and the challenges residents face when their communities are carved up. Click on any neighborhood to see how it’s divided, who represents it, and why advocates say the lines matter.
