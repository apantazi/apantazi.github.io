Arizona Legislative Compass

Explore every vote cast by Arizona lawmakers: The Legislative Compass is an interactive dashboard that helps you spot patterns in partisan loyalty, crossover votes, and how legislators compare to the politics and demographics of their districts. Filter by year, chamber, party, or district. The tool is designed for journalists and anyone curious about which lawmakers break ranks, who represents swing districts, and how lawmakers' voting records align with their electorate.
